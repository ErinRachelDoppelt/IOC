About Me

Erin Rachel Doppelt travels around the world leading numerous mindfulness workshops (conflict competence, emotional intelligence, nutrition, yoga, soul mapping and meditation) to create safe/welcoming spaces and train influencers or leaders in these practices- these workshops are derived from the teachings she learned while backpacking The Mid East and South East Asia. She is a MA candidate at Columbia University NYC studying Psychology as it relates to spiritual Mind and Body practices.
